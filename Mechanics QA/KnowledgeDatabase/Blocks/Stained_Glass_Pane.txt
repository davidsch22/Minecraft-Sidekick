Stained glass panes are a dyed version of regular glass panes that can be used for decoration purposes.
If a stained glass pane is broken with a tool enchanted with silk touch, it will drop itself; otherwise it will drop nothing.
Like regular panes, stained glass panes function as a much more efficient solution to stained glass, covering much more surface with less used resources.
