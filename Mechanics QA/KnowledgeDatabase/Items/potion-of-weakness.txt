Potions of weakness will reduce your damage output. A potion of weakness and a potion of strength will cancel out part of their effects, depending on the strength of the potions.
There are 2 types of potions of weakness. A 90 second version and a 4 minute version. A potion of weakness will reduce your damage output by 2 hearts.
