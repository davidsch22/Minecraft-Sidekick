Beetroot Soup is a type of food added in Pocket Edition version 0.8.0 and 1.9 PC. It can be crafted with 6 Beetroot and 1 Bowl. When eaten, four hunger will be restored, and the bowl will be given back, like with Mushroom Stew.
Pocket Edition Only.
Beetroot soup is a liquid food item.
