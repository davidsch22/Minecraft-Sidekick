Raw salmon is a food item that is obtained by fishing.
The most common way of obtaining raw salmon, like any other fish, is fishing. The chance of one being caught can be increased with the 'Lure' enchantment.
Raw salmon can be obtained from guardians as a rare drop.
One raw salmon restores 2 () hunger points and 0.4 saturation. Although it is more efficient to cook it in a furnace to produce cooked salmon, which restores 6 () hunger points and 9.6 saturation.
Raw Salmon can be used to tame ocelots, like most other fish.
