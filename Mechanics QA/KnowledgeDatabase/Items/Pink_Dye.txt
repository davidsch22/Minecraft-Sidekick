Pink dye is a dye made from Pink Tulips or Peonies, or by combining a Rose Red and one Bone Meal in a crafting square.
Like all other dyes, pink dye can be Applied to sheep to dye their wool, which can then be shorn for 1–3 blocks of pink wool.
Like all other dyes, pink dye can be Applied to tamed wolves to dye their collars.
Like all other dyes, pink dye can be Combined with gunpowder to make a firework star.
Like all other dyes, pink dye can be Combined with a Firework Star to create a “fade to color” effect.
Like all other dyes, pink dye can be Used to dye leather armor.
Like all other dyes, pink dye can be Used to dye wool, or stain glass or hardened clay.
Like all other dyes, pink dye can be Used to add patterns to banners.
Pink dye has the ID name minecraft:dye, with a data value of 9.
