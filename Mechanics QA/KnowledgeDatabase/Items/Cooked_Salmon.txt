Cooked salmon is obtained when raw salmon is cooked in a furnace. The cooked salmon's appearance resembles the raw salmon, but without the top fin and more orange in color.
Cooked Salmon can be eaten for 6 () hunger points and 9.6 saturation. As fishing can be done in any size body of water, this makes cooked salmon a good food source if the necessary resources can be obtained.
