Magenta dye is a dye made from combining one purple dye and a pink dye in a crafting square. It can also be made by placing an allium or a lilac in the crafting table.
Like all other dyes, magenta dye can be Applied to sheep to dye their wool, which can then be shorn for 1–3 blocks of magenta wool.
Like all other dyes, magenta dye can be Applied to tamed wolves to dye their collars.
Like all other dyes, magenta dye can be Combined with gunpowder to make a firework star.
Like all other dyes, magenta dye can be Combined with a Firework Star to create a “fade to color” effect.
Like all other dyes, magenta dye can be Used to dye leather armor.
Like all other dyes, magenta dye can be Used to dye wool, or stain glass or hardened clay.
Like all other dyes, magenta dye can be Used to add patterns to banners.
Magenta dye has the ID name minecraft:dye, with a data value of 13.
